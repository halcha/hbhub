What is HB HUB?

HB Hub is a platform for all members or as we like to call them ‘Players’, to share their opinion on a variety of topics, from financial services and current affairs to food and personal preferences.

Every time a player logs into their dashboard they have the opportunity to complete various activities including surveys, quizzes and polls in exchange for points which they can later cash in for rewards.

These rewards include exclusive offers, luxury prize draws, charitable donations and gift cards of a player’s choosing. Each interaction strengthens our relationship and understanding of each individual player, which in turn enables us to create the ultimate, curated and personalised experience for our members.
